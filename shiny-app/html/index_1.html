Introduction

Global warming caused by the emission of greenhouse gases will have enumerable effects on our way of life. In this project, I will explore how rising sea level is predicted to affect the demographics of the United States in the near future.

The questions that you will be able to answer using this Shiny dashboard include:

  • Which U.S. counties will have the most significant population shifts caused by Sea Level Rise?
  • What are the general trends in these population changes?
  • After adjusting for population, which U.S. counties will be most affected Sea Level Rise migration?
  • Is there a relaltionship between high net migration and other variables?
